About

Little Angels Child Care (LACC) is an in-home family child care service for children ages between four months to six years old located in Silver Spring, MD. LACC is licensed by the State of Maryland. Sofia Abera, the owner and service provider, earned her certification from Montgomery College for Child Growth and Development and Planning Curricula for Child Care. Sofia has more than six years of experience in child care. Her passion in life is to nurture children and to watch them succeed. At LACC, we believe in the importance of creating a welcoming environment that stimulates children to grow socially, emotionally, and intellectually. Our goal is to provide a safe, exciting, fun, and warm environment for children to thrive.
